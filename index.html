About Us

Our Mission

To empower women and youth with the knowledge, skills, and resources they need to build resilience against climate change while fostering sustainable development and peaceful communities. Through practical education, advocacy, and innovative climate-smart solutions, we create inclusive opportunities that strengthen environmental stewardship, improve livelihoods, and nurture hope for a better future.

Our Vision

Thriving, empowered communities where women and youth lead the way in building resilience to climate change, sustainably managing natural resources, and creating peaceful, prosperous futures for generations to come.

Core Focus Areas

  • 01 Sustainable Agriculture & Climate-Smart Practices
  • 02 Food Security & Nutrition
  • 03 Empowerment of Women and Youth
  • 04 Peacebuilding & Inclusive Communities
